Brief History
Kentionary is a platform that is useful in translating Kenyan languages. The idea was coined by Daniel Kaniu, who is the founder of Hallelujah Technologies Inc. (The parent company of Kentionary Inc. ).
At that time he was in University studying Bachelor of Business Information Technology.

The founder developed the application with the help of his programming coach, Professor Peter Muraya (A Computer Scientist Guru with over 40 years of experience). The development process was guided by the System Development Life Cycle steps:-
  1. Planning
  2. Analysis
  3. Design
  4. Development
  5. Testing
  6. Implementation
  7. Maintenance
Vision
We hope that our application will help people in Kenya and abroad to translate the local Kenyan languages. And they are all able to overcome the language barrier.
